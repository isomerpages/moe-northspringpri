School Term and Break Schedule
==============================

#### School Terms for 2026

| Semester 1 |  |
|---|---|
| Term 1 | Fri 2 Jan&nbsp;to Fri 13 Mar |
| Term 2 |Mon 23 Mar to Fri 29 May |

* Primary 1 and Kindergarten 1 will start school on Friday, 2 January 2026. Primary 2 to 6 and Kindergarten 2 will start school on Monday, 5 January 2026.

| Semester 2 |  |
|---|---|
| Term 3 | Mon 29 Jun to Fri 4 Sep |
| Term 4 | Mon 14 Sep to Fri 20 Nov |
|

#### School Holidays for 2026

|  |  |
|---|---|
| Between Terms I &amp; II | Sat 14 Mar to Sun 22 Mar |
| Between Semesters I &amp; II | Sat 30 May to Sun 28 Jun |
| Between Terms III &amp; IV | Sat 5 Sep to Sun 13 Sep |
| At End of School Year | Sat 21 Nov to Thu 31 Dec |
|
