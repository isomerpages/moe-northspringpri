<h4>Supporting Your Child's Digital Learning Journey</h4>
<p>Every student deserves the tools to connect, learn, and succeed.</p>
<p>We know that keeping up with technology is essential for your child's
education. To ensure no student is left behind, we are pleased to share
information about the national&nbsp;<strong>DigitalAccess@Home</strong>&nbsp;scheme.</p>
<p>This initiative, supported by IMDA, helps families who may benefit from
additional support by providing&nbsp;<strong>subsidised laptops/tablets and broadband access</strong>&nbsp;for
eligible students.</p>
<hr>
<p><strong>How the Scheme Can Help</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>A Device for Learning:</strong>&nbsp;A subsidised laptop or tablet
for your child to use for schoolwork.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Staying Connected:</strong>&nbsp;Subsidised broadband internet
to access online lessons and resources.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Peace of Mind:</strong>&nbsp;Ensuring your child has the same
opportunities as their classmates.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>3 Simple Steps to Get Started&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>1. Learn More &amp; Check Eligibility</strong>
<br>Find out if your family qualifies for the scheme and what support is available.
<br>👉&nbsp;<a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Visit the Official IMDA Page</a>
</p>
<p><strong>2. Complete the Application</strong>
<br>Download and fill out the application form. If you need a printed copy,
please contact the school office.
<br>📄&nbsp;<a href="/files/digitalaccess_application_form__1_.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Download the Application Form (PDF)</a>
</p>
<p><strong>3. Get Friendly, In-Person Help (If Needed)</strong>
<br>If you have questions or would like someone to guide you through the process,
you can visit an&nbsp;<strong>SG Digital Community Hub</strong>. A Digital
Ambassador will be happy to help you in a relaxed, no-pressure setting.
<br>📍&nbsp;<a href="https://www.digitalforlife.gov.sg/about/contact/sg-digital-community-hub" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Find a Hub Near You</a>
</p>
