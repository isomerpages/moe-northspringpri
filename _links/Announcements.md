<h4>The Inspiring Teacher of English Awards</h4>
<p>Dear Parents and Students,</p>
<p>The Inspiring Teacher of English Award is a special national honor for
English teachers in Singapore schools. It celebrates teachers who do a
fantastic job of making English fun and exciting, while also helping their
students speak and write really well.</p>
<p><strong>Nominations open on 1 Feb 2024 and all forms must reach the Speak Good English Movement by 5pm on 5 April 2024.</strong>
</p>
<p>Click on this link to download the nomination forms: <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.languagecouncils.sg/goodenglish/inspiring-teacher-of-english-award/nomination-information</a>
</p>
<p>Find out more on Youtube:&nbsp;<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.youtube.com/user/goodenglishmovement</a> and</p>
<p>Nominate our deserving teachers for the award!</p>
