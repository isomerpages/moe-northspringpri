Badminton
=========

Badminton is a sport enjoyed by many in Singapore. The inclusive nature of the CCA reflects the multi-racial and diverse cultural society that we live in. Values such as teamwork and sportsmanship are also taught and inculcated during training, equipping our students with essential life skills.
